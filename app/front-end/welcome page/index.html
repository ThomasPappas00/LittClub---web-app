A big community. Join for free.

Visiting this platform, the chances are that you love books. At LittClub you will find people that share the same passion for reading and you will make friends with true book-a-holics. Follow people and topics and discuss your favorite books and ideas in a warm community.

With a lot of themes. Choose your favorite.

People from all over the world have gathered to share thoughts and ideas. You will find discussions for every book gerne and type. Art, adventure, biographies, classics, comics, drama, fantasy, fiction, history, horror, romance, science, philosophy, poetry. There is no limit for the reader's appetite.

And most importantly, discussions! Find or start new topics.

Do you want to start a discussion for a book? Just start a new topic with the title of that book and the passionate readers will come to join. Or even invite your friends!
